About

Shift Money is a Financial Technology Conference, which purpose is to explore new ways to disrupt the world of Payments, Insurance, Lending & Blockchain. This event gathers the representatives of every sector of the industry, no matter their size or stage, in one place to find new partners, create connections and find new opportunities.
